Getting Started

Overview, user roles, and how to sign in for the first time

ExpenseTrack is a role-based expense management platform. Employees record daily expenses, Line Managers review and approve them, and Super Admins manage the entire system including user accounts.

👥 User Roles at a Glance
RoleAccessPrimary Responsibilities
Employee Own data only Submit expenses, attach receipts, track approval status, manage bank details
Admin / Line Manager Team data Review & approve/reject expenses, export reports, view hierarchy, bulk actions
Super Admin All data + system Everything Admin can do + create/edit/delete users, assign roles, manage line managers
🔐 Signing In

You can sign in using any of three identifiers — use whichever is easiest:

  • Email address — e.g. john@company.com
  • Employee number — e.g. EMP001
  • Mobile number — e.g. 9876543210
ℹ️
First-time login — If your account was created by a Super Admin, you will be prompted to set a new personal password on your very first sign-in. Choose something strong and unique.

👤

Employee Guide

Submit expenses, check status, manage your profile and bank details

Employee
📊 Your Dashboard

After signing in you land on your Dashboard. The four summary cards at the top show:

⏳
PendingSubmitted expenses still awaiting manager review.
✅
ApprovedExpenses that have cleared all approval levels.
❌
RejectedDeclined expenses — rejection reason visible on the card.
💰
Total AmountSum of all your approved reimbursable expenses.

Below the cards you will find a bar chart of your monthly spending, followed by a full history table with date and status filters.

➕ Adding a Daily Expense — Step by Step

Click Add Expense in the sidebar to open the Daily Tracking page.

  1. Select the dateChoose the date the expense was incurred. Defaults to today. You can back-date entries.
  2. Click "+ Add Expense Entry"A form row appears. You can add multiple entries for the same day before saving.
  3. Choose a CategorySelect: Travel, Food, Accommodation, Tools, Communication, or Other.
  4. Enter the Amount (₹)Type the exact amount in Indian Rupees. Decimal values are supported (e.g. 245.50).
  5. Add a Description (recommended)Briefly explain the expense — e.g. "Taxi to client site, Jaipur". This helps approvers verify the entry.
  6. Attach a Receipt PhotoTap the camera/file button to attach a JPG, PNG, or PDF of the bill. Strongly recommended for all expenses over ₹100.
  7. Click "Save Daily Entries"All rows are submitted together. A success confirmation appears and the entries immediately show in your history with Pending status.
💡
Multiple entries per day — You can add as many rows as needed before clicking Save. Useful when you have several receipts from one day (taxi + lunch + hotel).
⚠️
Expenses cannot be edited after submission. Double-check the amount, date, and category before saving. If you notice an error, ask your manager to reject the entry so you can resubmit correctly.

🧾 Viewing a Receipt

Each expense entry with a receipt shows a small thumbnail in the history table. Click the thumbnail or the "View Receipt" button to open the full image in a new tab.

💡
If the receipt does not open, your browser may be blocking pop-ups. Look for the blocked-popup icon in the address bar and choose "Always allow pop-ups from this site".
👤 My Profile & Bank Details

Navigate to My Profile to view your employee information and save bank details for reimbursement:

  • Your Employee ID, department, site, and assigned line manager
  • Your line manager's name and direct phone number (tap to call)
  • Bank Account Holder Name, Bank Name, IFSC Code, Account Number
🏦
Complete your bank details before submitting expenses. Reimbursements are processed to the account saved in your profile. Incorrect details may cause payment delays.
📅 Work Plans

The Work Plans section shows plans assigned to you by your manager — including work location, objectives, travel dates, and current plan status. You cannot create or edit plans; contact your manager to update them.

Line Manager / Admin Guide

Review expenses, approve or reject, browse the hierarchy, and export reports

Admin
📊 Admin Dashboard

The Admin dashboard provides a real-time overview of all team expenses. Summary cards at the top show:

  • Total Submitted — all expenses ever recorded across the team
  • Pending Review — expenses currently waiting for your action
  • Approved This Month — total approved reimbursable amount for the current month
  • Total Rejected — count of declined entries
🏗️ Hierarchy View — Department → Manager → Employee

The Employee History section uses a collapsible three-level tree to organise all expenses logically:

🏢
Level 1 — DepartmentColour-coded badge per department. Shows total expenses, pending count, and total amount.
👔
Level 2 — ManagerManager's employee count, pending count, and approved total under that manager.
👤
Level 3 — EmployeeEmployee name, emp code, status badge counts (pending / approved / rejected), and total amount.
📋
Expense DetailClick an employee row to expand their expenses, grouped by billing cycle (15th–14th monthly).

Navigating the hierarchy:

  • Click a Department row → expands / collapses all managers in that department
  • Click a Manager row → expands / collapses employees under that manager
  • Click an Employee row → opens their full expense detail panel
  • Expand All and Collapse All buttons affect every level simultaneously
  • Use the Hierarchy / List toggle in the filter bar to switch between the tree view and the classic flat table view
🔍
When a search or filter is active, matching nodes automatically expand so results are immediately visible — no manual clicking needed.
✅ Reviewing and Approving Expenses
  1. Open an employee's expense detailIn Hierarchy view, click the employee's row. In List view, expand the expense card for that employee.
  2. Inspect each entryCheck category, amount, date, description, and receipt thumbnail. Click View Receipt to open the bill in full.
  3. Add a comment (optional but recommended)Type a note in the comment field — recorded permanently in the audit trail and visible to the employee.
  4. Click Approve or RejectApproved entries move to L1 Approved (awaiting final L2 sign-off). Rejected entries are returned to the employee with your comment.
⚡
Bulk Approval — Select multiple expense checkboxes and use the floating Approve Selected / Reject Selected bar at the bottom of the screen to process many at once.
🔎 Filters & Search

The sticky filter bar at the top of Employee History offers:

  • Search — type any name or employee code to filter the hierarchy instantly
  • Department dropdown — narrow the view to one department
  • Manager dropdown — show only employees under a specific manager
  • Date range — filter by submission date (from / to)
  • Status — show only Pending, Approved, or Rejected entries
📥 Exporting Reports

Click the Export dropdown in the filter bar to download data:

  • Export Excel (.xlsx) — fully formatted spreadsheet, sorted oldest → newest by date
  • Export CSV — plain comma-separated file compatible with any spreadsheet tool

Exports include: Date, Employee Name, Emp Code, Department, Category, Amount (₹), Status, Submitted At, L1 Approved Date, L2 Approved Date, Notes, and Receipt URL.

💡
Apply filters before exporting to download only the data you need — e.g. one department's expenses for the current billing cycle.

Full Name✅ YesEmployee's legal name as on company records

✏️ Editing a User
  1. Go to Manage UsersClick "Manage Users" in the sidebar. All accounts appear as cards in a grid.
  2. Click the user's cardA detail modal opens showing all editable fields.
  3. Edit fields and saveClick Save Changes. The profile updates immediately.
🔑 Resetting a Password

In the user detail modal, enter a new temporary password in the New Password field and click Update Password. The user will be required to change it again on their next login.

🗑️ Deleting a User
⚠️
This action is permanent and cannot be undone. The user's login credentials are removed from authentication and their profile is deleted from the database. Their historical expense records are retained (anonymised) for audit purposes.
  1. Open the user's detail modalClick their card in Manage Users.
  2. Click "🗑️ Delete User"A red confirmation dialog appears with the user's name and a warning message.
  3. ConfirmClick "Yes, Delete User". The account is removed immediately. The card disappears from the grid.
🚫
You cannot delete your own account. The delete button is hidden when viewing your own user card to prevent accidental self-lockout.

Approval System

Two-level review, comments, audit trail, and bulk actions explained

Admin
Two-Level Approval — Why it Exists

ExpenseTrack enforces a mandatory two-level approval process to ensure accountability and prevent errors:

1️⃣
L1 — Line Manager The employee's direct manager performs the first review. They verify the expense is valid, properly documented, and within team budget guidelines.
2️⃣
L2 — Admin / Super Admin A senior approver performs the final check. This provides a second set of eyes, ensures cross-team consistency, and protects against errors before reimbursement.
💬 Approval Comments

Every approve or reject action includes an optional comment field. Use comments to:

  • Explain a rejection — e.g. "Receipt image is blurry, please resubmit with a clearer photo"
  • Set expectations — e.g. "Approved this time, but please keep meal expenses under ₹400"
  • Acknowledge context — e.g. "Emergency travel approved by director"

Comments are permanent and visible to both the employee and all approvers in the audit trail.

⚡ Bulk Actions
  1. Enable selection modeClick the checkbox on any expense card or table row. A floating action bar appears at the bottom of the screen.
  2. Select more entriesContinue checking more expenses. The counter in the action bar updates as you select.
  3. Approve or Reject all selectedClick Approve Selected or Reject Selected. All checked entries update simultaneously.
⚠️
Bulk actions apply the same status to all selected items without per-item comments. Use individual approval for entries that require a specific explanation or policy note.
📋 Audit Trail

Every approval or rejection is permanently recorded with:

  • Exact timestamp of the action (date and time in IST)
  • Approver name (L1 or L2 level)
  • Comment text (if provided)

The audit trail is visible in each expense's detail panel and is included in all Excel / CSV exports.

Realtime Updates

How the dashboard stays live without manual page refreshes

ExpenseTrack uses Supabase Realtime — a WebSocket push channel — so dashboards update automatically the moment data changes anywhere in the system. No manual refreshes needed during a working session.

What triggers a live update?
📝
New expense submittedAll admin dashboards immediately show the new entry. Employee's own counter increments.
✅
Expense approved or rejectedThe employee's card status updates within seconds. Admin pending counters refresh too.
💬
Comment addedThe comment appears in the expense detail panel immediately after the approver saves it.
👤
Profile updatedDepartment or role changes take effect on the next page navigation by that user.
📶 Offline Behaviour

If the internet connection is lost:

  • Live updates pause — the dashboard freezes at the last known state
  • Previously loaded data stays visible via the service worker cache
  • Updates resume automatically once the connection is restored
🔄 Manual Refresh Options
  • Press F5 or Ctrl + R (Windows) / Cmd + R (Mac) to reload
  • Pull-to-refresh on mobile (when the app is installed as a PWA)
  • The app also auto-reloads data when you switch browser tabs back to it
ℹ️
The realtime connection is active on the Dashboard page only. Other pages (Add Expense, Profile, Help, etc.) load fresh data on each navigation but do not maintain a live socket while open.

PWA / Mobile App

Install ExpenseTrack as a native-like app on any device — no app store required

ExpenseTrack is a Progressive Web App (PWA). You can install it on your phone or computer and launch it like any native app — with a home-screen icon, full-screen mode, and offline support — without visiting the Play Store or App Store.

  1. Open Chrome on AndroidNavigate to stockwell-expense.vercel.app and sign in to your account.
  2. Tap the three-dot menu (⋮)Located in the top-right corner of Chrome.
  3. Tap "Add to Home screen"Chrome shows a preview of the ExpenseTrack icon and name.
  4. Tap "Add"The icon appears on your home screen. Tap it to launch as a full-screen app with no browser chrome.
💡
Many Android devices show an automatic "Install App" banner at the bottom of the page. Tap it for a one-tap install without opening the menu.
  1. Open Safari on iPhone or iPadPWA installation only works in Safari on iOS — not in Chrome or other browsers.
  2. Navigate to the appGo to stockwell-expense.vercel.app and sign in.
  3. Tap the Share button (□↑)Found at the bottom of the screen on iPhone, or at the top on iPad.
  4. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"Edit the display name if you wish, then tap Add in the top-right corner.
🍎
On iOS 16.4 and later, you can receive push notifications from the installed app. Tap Allow when prompted to stay updated on expense approvals.
  1. Open Chrome or Microsoft EdgeNavigate to stockwell-expense.vercel.app and sign in.
  2. Look for the install icon (⊕)An install icon appears in the right side of the browser address bar.
  3. Click the icon and then "Install"A small confirmation dialog appears. Click Install to proceed.
  4. Launch from Start / ApplicationsExpenseTrack now opens as a standalone window — find it in your Start menu (Windows) or Applications folder (Mac).
📶 Offline Support

The service worker caches the app shell (HTML, CSS, JavaScript) so the interface loads instantly even without a network connection. However:

  • Expense data requires a live connection — offline shows the last-loaded snapshot only
  • New expenses cannot be submitted while offline
  • An offline notice appears if the server cannot be reached
🔔 Automatic App Updates

When a new version of ExpenseTrack is deployed:

  • An "Updating to latest version…" toast slides up at the bottom of the screen
  • The app reloads automatically after ~2 seconds to apply the update
  • No manual action is needed — updates are seamless
🔄
The app checks for updates every 30 seconds and whenever you switch back to the tab. If you see stale data, switch away and return — an update may be in progress.

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions from all user roles

Can I edit or delete an expense after submitting?
No — expenses cannot be edited or deleted by the employee after submission. If you made an error, contact your Line Manager or Admin and ask them to reject the entry with a note. You can then resubmit the corrected expense.
What receipt file formats are accepted?
Receipts can be uploaded as:
  • Images — JPG, JPEG, PNG, WebP, HEIC (iPhone photos)
  • Documents — PDF
Maximum file size is 10 MB per receipt. Standard phone photos are well within this limit.
Why can't I see my receipt when I click "View Receipt"?
This is almost always a browser pop-up blocker. To fix it:
  • Look for a pop-up blocked icon in your browser's address bar
  • Click it and choose "Always allow pop-ups from this site"
  • Click "View Receipt" again — the image will open in a new tab
On mobile, pop-up blockers are rare. If you still cannot see the receipt, try a page refresh.
How long does the approval process take?
Approval timelines depend on your company's internal policy. ExpenseTrack does not enforce a time limit, but sends real-time notifications to approvers when new expenses are submitted. If an expense has been pending for more than 2 business days, contact your Line Manager directly using the phone number on your Profile page.
Who can see my expense entries?
Your expenses are visible to:
  • You — always, on your own Dashboard
  • Your Line Manager and Admins — for review and approval purposes
  • Super Admin — full system-wide access
Other employees at the same level cannot see your expense data.
Can I export my own expense history?
Currently, export functionality is available to Admin and Super Admin roles only. If you need a personal copy of your history, ask your Admin to filter by your employee ID and export the data for you.
How do I change my password?
  • If you were prompted to change your password on first login, follow that flow
  • To change it at any other time, ask your Super Admin to reset your password from the Manage Users page — you will then be required to set a new one on your next login
A self-service password reset feature is planned for a future update.
I see old data even after refreshing. What should I do?
The service worker may be serving a cached version. Try these steps in order:
  • Switch to another browser tab and come back — the app checks for updates on tab focus
  • Hard refresh: Ctrl + Shift + R (Windows) or Cmd + Shift + R (Mac)
  • Clear site cache: Chrome → Settings → Privacy & Security → Clear browsing data → Cached images and files
The app is slow or won't load on mobile. How do I fix this?
Try these steps:
  • Check your internet connection — ExpenseTrack requires a live connection to load expense data
  • Close and reopen the app (if installed as a PWA on your home screen)
  • Clear the app cache: long-press the app icon → App Info → Storage → Clear Cache
  • Reinstall the PWA: remove the home screen icon and re-add it from the browser
How are billing cycles calculated?
Expenses in the admin hierarchy view are grouped into billing cycles that run from the 15th to the 14th of the following month. For example:
  • 15 Apr – 14 May → "Apr–May Cycle"
  • 15 May – 14 Jun → "May–Jun Cycle"
This grouping makes it easy for admins to batch-approve a complete period's submissions in one go.
What happens to an employee's expenses if their account is deleted?
When a Super Admin deletes a user account, all historical expense records are retained in the database for audit purposes. The records are detached from the deleted user profile (shown as "Unknown Employee" in reports) but the financial data is never erased. This ensures complete audit trail integrity.
